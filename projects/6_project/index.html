Multi-Layer PCB Laboratory

Management of PCB fabrication facility for defense applications

Role Overview

Served as Manager of the Multi-Layer PCB Laboratory at the Egyptian Armed Forces Research Center, overseeing the complete PCB fabrication process for complex defense and research electronics projects.

Laboratory Capabilities

PCB Specifications

  • Layer Count: Up to 8 layers
  • Minimum Track/Space: 4mil/4mil (0.1mm)
  • Minimum Via Size: 8mil (0.2mm) diameter
  • Board Size: Up to 500mm x 600mm
  • Copper Weight: 1oz to 3oz
  • Surface Finish: HASL, ENIG, OSP

Manufacturing Processes

  • Imaging: Direct laser imaging (DLI) for high-precision patterns
  • Etching: Automated spray etching system
  • Drilling: CNC drilling with micro-drill capability
  • Plating: Through-hole plating and surface finishing
  • Solder Mask: LPI (Liquid Photo-Imageable) solder mask
  • Legend: Silkscreen printing for component identification

Quality Assurance

Testing & Inspection

  • Electrical Testing: Flying probe and bed-of-nails testers
  • Visual Inspection: AOI (Automated Optical Inspection)
  • Dimensional Accuracy: Coordinate measuring machine (CMM)
  • Cross-sectioning: Microsectioning for layer alignment verification
  • Impedance Testing: TDR for controlled impedance verification

Quality Standards

  • Implemented IPC-A-600 (Acceptability of Printed Boards)
  • IPC-6012 (Qualification and Performance Specification)
  • ISO 9001 quality management procedures
  • Traceability and documentation for all production batches

Key Achievements

  • Production Volume: Managed fabrication of 500+ PCB designs annually
  • Yield Improvement: Increased first-pass yield from 85% to 95%
  • Lead Time Reduction: Reduced average turnaround time by 30%
  • Team Management: Led team of 12 technicians and engineers
  • Process Optimization: Implemented lean manufacturing principles

Projects Supported

  • Radar Systems: High-frequency RF PCBs with controlled impedance
  • Communication Equipment: Multi-layer boards with complex routing
  • Control Systems: Industrial-grade controllers with high reliability
  • Sensor Modules: Precision analog circuits with low noise requirements
  • Power Electronics: High-current boards with thermal management

Technical Expertise Developed

  • Advanced PCB stackup design and impedance control
  • DFM (Design for Manufacturing) guidelines and reviews
  • Failure analysis and root cause investigation
  • Process parameter optimization
  • Equipment calibration and maintenance
  • Staff training and skill development

Technologies & Equipment

CAD Tools: Altium Designer, CAM350
Fabrication: Laser imaging, CNC drilling, Automated etching
Testing: Flying probe, AOI, Impedance analyzer
Quality: Microsectioning, CMM, Environmental testing
